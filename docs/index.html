The loop

Hold. Speak. Release.

No window to open. No button to find. It lives in your menu bar and works in every app you already use.

  1. 01

    Hold a key

    Press and hold Fn — or your own shortcut. A quiet pill appears and never steals focus.

  2. 02

    Speak your mind

    Talk naturally, at the speed of thought. Your words stream in live as you go.

  3. 03

    Release

    Let go. In about a second, clean, finished text lands right where your cursor was.

Private by construction

Your voice never leaves your Mac.

Audio is captured, transcribed, and cleaned entirely on your device, then discarded from memory. No account. No telemetry. No cloud.

The only network connection LocalFlow ever makes is a one-time model download. Watch it with Little Snitch — after that, silence.

  • On-device speech-to-text on the Neural Engine
  • On-device AI cleanup (Apple Intelligence or local Ollama)
  • History stored only on your Mac — or not at all
  • Two permissions, ever: Microphone & Accessibility

Everything stays here.

Get started

Up and running in minutes.

  1. Download LocalFlow.dmg from Releases, open it, and drag LocalFlow into Applications.
  2. First launch: right-click → Open (it's an unsigned build for now).
  3. Grant Microphone & Accessibility when onboarding asks — the only two permissions.
  4. Dictate immediately while the speech model downloads once in the background.
